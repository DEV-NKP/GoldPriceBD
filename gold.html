Gold in Bangladesh — Complete Guide 2026

Complete Guide | Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

22 Karat Gold Price Bangladesh 2026

22 Karat (22K) gold contains 91.67% pure gold and is the most popular standard for gold jewellery in Bangladesh. The BAJUS rate for 22K gold is updated daily — check the live rates above for today's exact price per vori and per gram. Prices change when BAJUS issues new announcements, typically 1-3 times per week.

21 Karat Gold in Bangladesh — Hallmark Standard

21 Karat gold (87.5% pure) is the hallmark standard used in Bangladesh. BAJUS officially certifies 21K gold jewellery and sets its daily rate proportionally below 22K pricing. 21K gold price BD is typically about 5% less than 22K per vori.

18 Karat Gold — Diamond & Stone Settings

18 Karat gold (75% pure) is used for diamond and precious stone jewellery in Bangladesh. More durable than higher-karat gold, it's preferred for rings and pendants with gemstones. 18K gold price BD is typically about 18% less than 22K per vori.

Traditional (Sanatan) Gold Price BD

Traditional or Sanatan gold refers to old recycled gold of variable purity (approximately 60%). This is the lowest tier as purity is not certified. Check the live rates above for today's Traditional gold price per vori and per gram.

Gold Measurement Units in Bangladesh — 1 Vori = ?

Bangladesh uses the Vori (ভরি) = 11.664 grams as the primary gold measurement unit. 1 Vori = 1 Tola = 16 Ana = 96 Roti = 960 Point. Gold is also traded per gram for international transactions. Our gold calculator supports all units including troy ounce (31.1035g).

Gold Jewellery Total Cost in Bangladesh — Calculation

Total jewellery cost = (BAJUS gold price × weight in grams) + making charge (≈৳300/gram for gold) + 5% VAT on the gold value. Use our gold calculator for an instant accurate estimate with today's BAJUS rates.
