About Gold & Silver Prices in Bangladesh

Complete Guide | Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

What is BAJUS?

BAJUS (Bangladesh Jewellers Samity — বাংলাদেশ জুয়েলার্স সমিতি) is the official trade association of gold and silver jewellers in Bangladesh. They publish the official daily gold and silver rates that all registered jewellers must follow. Their office is at Bashundhara City Shopping Complex, Dhaka.

What is 1 Vori (ভরি)?

1 Vori (also called Tola or Bhori) equals 11.664 grams and is the primary unit for gold and silver measurement in Bangladesh. 1 Vori = 16 Ana = 96 Roti = 960 Point. It is equivalent to 1 Tola used in India and Pakistan.

Gold Karats in Bangladesh

22K (91.67% pure) — Most popular for jewellery in Bangladesh. 21K (87.5%) — Hallmark standard. 18K (75%) — Used for diamond/stone settings. Traditional/Sanatan — Old jewellery, approximately 60% pure, no fixed standard.

How to Calculate Jewellery Cost

Total cost = (BAJUS gold rate × weight in grams) + making charge (≈৳300/gram for gold, ৳26/gram for silver) + 5% VAT on the gold value. Use our calculator above for an instant accurate estimate.

আজকের সোনার দাম বাংলাদেশ ২০২৬

বাংলাদেশে সোনার দাম প্রতিদিন BAJUS (বাংলাদেশ জুয়েলার্স সমিতি) দ্বারা নির্ধারিত হয়। আজকের ২২ ক্যারেট সোনার দাম প্রতি ভরি প্রায় ৳২,৪৪,৭১১ টাকা। আন্তর্জাতিক গোল্ড স্পট প্রাইস ও USD/BDT বিনিময় হারের উপর ভিত্তি করে দাম পরিবর্তন হয়।

As of April 2026, the BAJUS silver price in Bangladesh is ৳5,365 per vori for 22K silver. 21K silver is ৳5,132/vori and 18K is ৳4,432/vori. Silver making charges are approximately ৳26/gram — much lower than gold. Use our calculator for total jewellery cost.

Gold Rate Factors in Bangladesh 2026

Gold prices in Bangladesh are influenced by: international XAU spot price, USD/BDT exchange rate, import duties & VAT, BAJUS margins, and local supply/demand. In 2026, prices have increased significantly due to global uncertainty and central bank buying.
