Silver in Bangladesh — Complete Guide 2026

Complete Guide | Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

22 Karat Silver Price Bangladesh 2026

22 Karat (22K) silver contains 91.67% pure silver. As of April 2026, the BAJUS rate is ৳5,365 per vori (approximately ৳460/gram). This is the most popular silver standard for jewellery in Bangladesh.

21 Karat Silver in Bangladesh

21 Karat silver (87.5% pure). As of April 2026, 21K silver costs ৳5,132 per vori. BAJUS officially certifies 21K silver and sets rates proportionally below 22K pricing.

18 Karat Silver — Stone Settings

18 Karat silver (75% pure) costs ৳4,432 per vori in Bangladesh as of April 2026. More durable and popular for gemstone settings like rings, pendants, and bracelets with precious stones.

Traditional (Sanatan) Silver

Traditional silver in Bangladesh costs ৳3,324 per vori (April 2026). This refers to old recycled silver of variable purity (~60%). The lowest BAJUS tier with no guaranteed purity certification.

Silver vs Gold — Bangladesh 2026

Silver is significantly cheaper than gold, making it accessible to wider buyers. The gold/silver ratio in 2026 is approximately 80-100:1. When the ratio exceeds 80, silver is historically undervalued — potentially a buying opportunity. Check our live ratio tracker above.

Silver Jewellery Total Cost in Bangladesh

Total cost = (BAJUS silver rate × weight in grams) + making charge (≈৳26/gram) + 5% VAT. Example: 50g of 22K silver at ৳460/g = ৳23,000 silver + ৳1,300 making + ৳1,150 VAT = ৳25,450 total. Use our calculator above.
